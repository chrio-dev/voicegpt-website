Frequently Asked Questions

How can I enable the GPT skill on my Amazon device?

To enable the GPT skill on your Amazon device, open the Alexa app, search for "GPT" to enable the skill, and say, "Alexa, start GPT" to start using it.

How can GPT improve my daily tasks?

GPT can streamline your daily tasks by providing quick answers, setting reminders, and managing your schedule efficiently. It integrates with smart home devices to control lights, thermostats, and more, making your life easier and more organized.

What are the benefits of using the GPT Amazon skill?

The novel Alexa skill will turn all your Amazon voice devices such as Alexa into your personal assistance which is 24/7 available. Use the GPT skill to work on any of for projects and have lenghty and in-depth dialogues to get all your problems solved.

What other features does the GPT Amazon skill offer?

Alex GPT Pro will offer you unlimited dialogues, GPT-4o and conversation export features. For our newest GPT Pro features please visit our blog post page.

What are the privacy and security measures for the GPT Amazon skill?

All your data remains secure. Personal data or conversations are not stored permanently.

What are the system requirements for using the GPT Amazon skill?

To use the Amazon Alexa skill GPT you need any Alexa device or use it on your phone, window or MAC computer.
